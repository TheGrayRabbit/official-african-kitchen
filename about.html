About Us

Welcome to African Kitchen

African Kitchen was founded by our illustrious chef Fay in late 2019, few months before the first lockdown. Throughout the lockdown, we delivered and sustained over 200 people in the Norfolk area. Since then, we have catered for thousands of people through our personalised meals and events. We have been trusted by our customers to cater for their specials days such as Weddings, Christenings, Birthdays and built strong corporate relationship with the likes of NHS, UEA and others. Our food is made of authentic flavors, African spices, and ingredients. We pride ourselves in delivering quality produce and excellent services.
